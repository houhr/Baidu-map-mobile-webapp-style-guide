7 卡片

组成页面布局的基本结构

包括card和container两种基本容器

card 和 container 的区别在于card四周都有border,container默认没有border

7.1 卡片样式

类名: card

可选参数：

-no-padding 去掉padding

normal card
no-padding card
<div id="example-7-1">
    <div class="card">
        <div class="card -light">normal card</div>
        <div class="card -light -no-padding"> no-padding card</div>
    </div>
</div>

7.2 卡片颜色

默认 继承父级元素

可选参数：

-lighter
-light
-normal
-dark

lighter card
light card
normal card
dark card
<div id="example-7-2">
    <div class="card -lighter">lighter card</div>
    <div class="card -light">light card</div>
    <div class="card -normal">normal card</div>
    <div class="card -dark">dark card</div>
</div>

7.3 容器样式

类名：container

默认

可选参数：

-no-padding 去掉padding

-border 添加border-bottom

normal container
no-padding container
border container
<div id="example-7-1">
    <div class="container -light">
        <div class="container -lighter ">normal container</div>
        <div class="container -normal -no-padding"> no-padding container</div>
        <div class="container -dark -border"> border container</div>
    </div>
</div>

7.4 容器颜色

颜色定义和卡片相同

lighter container
light container
normal container
dark container
<div id="example-7-4">
    <div class="container -lighter">lighter container</div>
    <div class="container -light">light container</div>
    <div class="container -normal">normal container</div>
    <div class="container -dark">dark container</div>
</div>
